Avatar Cam is a desktop application that displays personalized 2D avatars with interactivity to enhance engagement and personal brand in online media.

What we can do for you:

- Your own 2D avatars in any perspective.
- Maps real-time user activity to avatar emotes from mouse, keyboard, controller, microphone, and (NEW) Twitch service!.
- Easy embedding with broadcasting software for maximum performance and compatibility.

In-depth feature list:

- Multi-profile setup and seamless transition between them.
- Toggable UI to change setting for various features such as microphone input.
- All of mouse, keyboard, controller, microphone, and online services can act as a hotkey to trigger emotes.
- Animated emote with transparency support.

We provide a complete package for your commission. All you have to do is run the application and see your avatar in action!

Our cams are designed to be efficient and stays out of the way during your activities. Resource consumption will be almost zero, and the cams will be rendered as a tiny game for software compatibility.

Available on Windows and Linux
